Student Label

Student Label brings game changing concept of the school apparel in the Czech Republic. No more uniforms or printed hoodies. SL aims to deliver fashionable clothing lines with student life or school motives. Project has evolved from e-shop offering hot picks from SL designers to platform which connects SL designers and creative officers with school representatives among students (ambassadors). The new platform helps to cooperate and create stylish and tailored school apparel. www.studentlabel.cz
